Have you ever shared a link on WhatsApp and noticed that only the URL appears, without an image or a title? Or perhaps you've shared a link on X (Twitter) only to find the image is awkwardly cropped?

In the digital-first economy of 2026, **the link is your business card.** If your social share preview doesn't look professional, people simply won't click.

## The Psychology of the "Preview Card"

Humans are visual creatures. A shared link with a high-quality **1200x630 OG image** and a compelling title creates "Social Proof." It tells the recipient that the content is legitimate, professional, and worth their time.

### The 3 Mistakes Killing Your Traffic

1.  **Generic Images**: Using your logo as the OG image for every page is a missed opportunity. Every article should have its own context-specific image.
2.  **Too Much Text**: If your title exceeds **60 characters**, social platforms will cut it off with an ellipsis (...), hiding your call to action.
3.  **Ignoring WhatsApp**: In Pakistan and many other markets, WhatsApp is the primary driver of viral content. If your preview fails there, you lose a massive chunk of potential reach.

## How to Debug Your Link Previews

Testing manually by sending links to yourself is slow and annoying. Instead, you should use a **Social Meta Tag Debugger**. 

These tools allow you to:
*   See exactly what your link looks like on different screen sizes.
*   Check if your image is loading correctly.
*   Verify that your description is engaging and not cut off.
