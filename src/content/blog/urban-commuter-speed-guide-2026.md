### 1. Does the Train Speedometer work on underground subways?
Partially. If the subway has "Repeater" nodes (common in 2026 smart cities), your phone can use Wi-Fi Positioning (WPS) to estimate speed. However, for true GPS accuracy, the tool works best on overground rail sections.

### 2. Is it safe to mount my phone on my bike?
Yes, but use a high-quality vibration-dampening mount. In 2026, smartphone cameras have delicate OIS (Optical Image Stabilization) systems that can be damaged by high-frequency road vibrations if the mount is too rigid.

### 3. Why does my e-scooter speed drop on hills?
Most e-scooter motors are limited by "Torque." As you climb, the motor draws more current but produces less RPM. Our GPS tool will show the exact drop in velocity, helping you decide if you need to "Kick-assist" to save your battery.
