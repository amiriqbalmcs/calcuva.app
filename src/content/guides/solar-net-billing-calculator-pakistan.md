## How to Use the Solar Net Billing Calculator

This tool helps you understand your monthly electricity bill reduction under the 2026 NEPRA rules.

## Key Inputs
1. **Monthly Generation**: The total units (kWh) your panels produce in a month.
2. **Import Rate**: The price you pay for grid electricity (typically Rs. 55-65 in 2026).
3. **Export Rate**: The "Buyback" rate your utility pays you for surplus units.
4. **Self-Consumption**: The percentage of solar energy you use directly at home without sending it to the grid.

## Understanding the Results
*   **Direct Savings**: The money saved by NOT buying units from the grid.
*   **Grid Credits**: The balance added to your bill for units you exported.
*   **Net Benefit**: Your total bill reduction after fixed charges.
