#### Metric vs. Imperial: The Decimal Advantage
The Metric (SI) system was designed in 1795 to be a "system for all time." Everything is based on powers of 10. 
*   **Metric**: 100 Centimeters = 1 Meter | 1,000 Meters = 1 Kilometer.
*   **Imperial**: 12 Inches = 1 Foot | 3 Feet = 1 Yard | 1,760 Yards = 1 Mile.

Because Imperial units are not base-10, they often lead to "Conversion Friction" in engineering and logistics. ## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Why is a US Gallon different from a UK Gallon?**
**A:** This is a historical anomaly. A **US Gallon** is 3.785 Liters, while a **UK (Imperial) Gallon** is 4.546 Liters. If you are following a recipe or a car manual, check the country of origin to avoid a 20% error.

**Q: What is a "Nautical Mile"?**
**A:** A nautical mile (1.852 km) is based on the circumference of the Earth and is equal to one minute of latitude. It is the global standard for air and sea navigation in 2026.

**Q: How do I convert Watts to Amps?**
**A:** You need the Voltage. The formula is `Amps = Watts / Volts`. For a standard 220V Pakistani outlet, a 1000W iron uses roughly 4.54 Amps.
