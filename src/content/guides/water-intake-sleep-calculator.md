#### The Math of Hydration: Solvent of Life
Water is the primary solvent for all biochemical reactions in your body. It regulates temperature, lubricates joints, and transports nutrients. 
*   **The Baseline**: We recommend 30–35ml of water per kg of body weight.
*   **The Activity Multiplier**: For every 30 minutes of intense exercise, you should add ~350ml (12oz) of fluid to compensate for sweat loss.

#### Expert Strategy: The 90-Minute Sleep Cycle Masterclass
Sleep is not a "Linear" process; it is a **Cyclical** one. 
1. **Light Sleep**: Preparing the body for rest.
2. **Deep Sleep**: Physical repair, hormone release, and immune boosting.
3. **REM (Rapid Eye Movement)**: Cognitive processing and memory consolidation.
By scheduling your wake-up time in **90-minute increments**, you ensure you are waking up during "Light Sleep." This eliminates **Sleep Inertia**—that groggy, heavy feeling that persists for hours after a bad wake-up.

#### Case Study: The "Sleep Debt" Myth
Many high-performers believe they can "hack" sleep by sleeping 5 hours on weekdays and 12 hours on weekends. 
**The Reality**: This creates "Social Jetlag." Your body's internal clock (The Circadian Rhythm) becomes confused, leading to metabolic issues and decreased focus. A consistent 7.5 hours (5 cycles) every night is significantly more restorative than an inconsistent 40 hours a week. #### The Circadian Rhythm: Light and Darkness
Your body's restoration is governed by **Melatonin** and **Cortisol**. 
*   **Evening**: Blue light from screens suppresses melatonin, making it harder to enter Deep Sleep. 
*   **Morning**: Natural light signals cortisol to wake you up.
