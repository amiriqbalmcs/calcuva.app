#### The Complexity of Month Addition
Adding "1 Month" is more complex than it sounds. 
*   **The Problem**: If the date is January 31st and you add 1 month, you should land on February 28th (or 29th). 
*   **The Logic**: Our engine uses a "Date Normalization" algorithm. If the target month has fewer days than the start day, we automatically roll the result to the final valid day of that month. This ensures you never end up with an "invalid" date like February 30th.
