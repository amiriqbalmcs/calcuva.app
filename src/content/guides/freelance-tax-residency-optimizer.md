## Freelance Tax & ROI Optimizer: User Guide

Maximize your hard-earned USD income by navigating the 2026 tax and banking landscape.

## Inputs Explained
*   **Monthly Income**: Your gross earnings in USD before any platform fees.
*   **Exchange Rate**: The current market or interbank rate.
*   **Filer Status**: Critical for calculating your FBR income tax deduction.
*   **Platform Fee**: The percentage taken by your marketplace (e.g., 10% for Upwork).

## Optimization Tips
*   **SECP vs Sole Prop**: If you earn consistently high amounts, consider registering as a company to optimize corporate taxes and formalize your business.
*   **PSEB Registration**: Ensure you are registered with the Pakistan Software Export Board to qualify for the 1% concessionary tax rate.
