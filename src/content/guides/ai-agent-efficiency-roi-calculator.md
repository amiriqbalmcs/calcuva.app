## Measuring AI ROI: A Strategic Guide

Stop guessing if your AI spend is worth it. Use this tool to get concrete dollar-value metrics.

## Methodology
1. **Labor Replacement Value**: We calculate how much it would cost to pay a human to do the work that the AI is now handling.
2. **Subscription Offset**: We subtract the direct costs of the AI (API fees, subscriptions) from the labor value saved.
3. **Team Multiplier**: We scale these savings across your entire team to show the cumulative organizational impact.

## Optimization Advice
*   **Flagship Models (Claude Opus / GPT-5)**: Use these for high-value reasoning where human labor is very expensive ($50+/hr).
*   **Mini Models (GPT-4o mini / Haiku)**: Use these for high-volume, low-complexity automation where the "cost per task" needs to be near zero.
