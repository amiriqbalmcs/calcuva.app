### 1. Decoding the ROI (Return on Investment)

In the crypto world, ROI is often expressed in "X's" (e.g., a 10x gain). While these numbers sound exhilarating, the underlying math is unforgiving.

*   **The Power of Percentages**: A 10x gain is actually a **900% increase**. 
*   **The Asymmetry of Loss**: This is the most important concept in crypto risk management. If a coin drops 50%, it needs a 100% increase (a 2x) just to recover your original entry point. If it drops **90%**, it doesn't just need a 90% increase—it needs a **900% (10x)** increase just to break even.

### 2. Expert Strategy: The "Scaled Exit" Masterclass

The biggest mistake retail investors make is "All or Nothing" thinking—waiting for the absolute "top" to sell their entire bag. Professionals use **Scaled Profit Taking**.

**The Strategy**:
1.  **Phase 1: Seed Recovery**: Sell enough of your position when the asset reaches a specific milestone (e.g., a 2x or 3x) to recover your **Original Capital**.
2.  **Phase 2: House Money**: Once your seed capital is back in your stablecoin wallet, you are playing with "House Money." You can now afford to hold the remaining 50% through massive volatility without the stress of losing your hard-earned savings.
3.  **Phase 3: The Moon Bag**: Keep a final 10-20% for "Infinite Upside." This is the portion you only sell if the asset reaches a generational milestone (e.g., a 100x).

### 3. Case Study: The "Hidden" Erosion of Fees

On decentralized exchanges (DEXs) like Uniswap or premium retail apps, fees are often "invisible" until it's too late.
*   **Trade Volume**: $1,000

*   **Network Gas (Ethereum)**: $25
*   **Platform Commission**: 0.5% ($5)

Your "Net Investment" is actually **$955**. To simply "Break Even," the asset must rise by **4.7%**, not 0%. If you are a high-frequency trader, these fees can compound into a 20% loss over a year. Always account for these network costs in our calculator to find your true "Net Profit" objective.
