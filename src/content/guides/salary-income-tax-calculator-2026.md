## Understanding the New FBR Tax Slabs (FY 2025-2026)

The Federal Board of Revenue (FBR) has implemented significant changes to the income tax structure for salaried individuals in Pakistan for the fiscal year 2025-26. These changes aim to increase revenue while providing a structured progressive tax system.

### Key Highlights of the 2025-26 Tax Regime

1. **Exemption Threshold**: The first Rs. 600,000 of your annual income is completely tax-free.
2. **Progressive Slabs**: As your income increases, the tax percentage applied to the "excess" amount also increases.
3. **Monthly vs. Annual**: While FBR defines taxes annually, our tool breaks it down into monthly deductions to help you manage your monthly budget.

### How to Calculate Your Tax

To get an accurate result, simply enter your **Gross Monthly Salary**. Our simulator will automatically:
- Calculate your **Annual Taxable Income**.
- Apply the correct **Progressive Slab** based on the 2025-26 Finance Act.
- Deduct the tax to show your **Monthly Take-Home Pay**.

### Example: Salary of Rs. 189,000
For an employee earning Rs. 189,000 per month:
- **Annual Income**: Rs. 2,268,000
- **Base Tax**: Rs. 116,000 (Fixed for the 2.2M bracket)
- **Excess Tax**: 23% on the amount exceeding Rs. 2,200,000
- **Total Monthly Tax**: Rs. 10,970
