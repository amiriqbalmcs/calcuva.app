### Master the Admission Game
Securing admission into Pakistan's top engineering and medical universities is less about intelligence and more about **strategic weightage management**. Understanding exactly where your points come from allows you to prioritize your study time effectively.

#### The Three Core Components
1. **The Foundation (Matric/SSC)**: Usually carries the least weight (10-15%), but acts as a safety net.
2. **The Bridge (Intermediate/HSSC)**: Carries moderate weight (15-40%). Many universities use Part-1 marks if Part-2 results are pending.
3. **The Decider (Entry Test)**: This is where most students win or lose. With a weightage of up to 75%, a high score here is the single most important factor for success.

#### Expert Admission Tactics
*   **The 75% Rule**: For universities like NUST, your NET score is 7.5x more important than your Matric marks. If your academic record is average, focus 90% of your energy on the entry test.
*   **Medical Merit (MDCAT)**: For medical colleges, the weightage is often split 50/50 between MDCAT and Intermediate. This requires a very high score in both to secure a seat in a government college.
*   **Custom Calculation**: Different departments within the same university might have different weightage. Use our **Custom Weights** mode to calculate specific merit for niche programs.
