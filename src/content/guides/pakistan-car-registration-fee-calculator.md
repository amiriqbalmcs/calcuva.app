## New Car Registration in Pakistan (2026)

Buying a new car is a major milestone, but the registration process involves navigating both federal and provincial tax systems. In 2026, the gap between Filers and Non-Filers has reached an all-time high, making it essential to understand the costs before you visit the dealership.

### The Three Major Cost Components

1. **Federal Withholding Tax (Advance Tax)**
This is a one-time tax collected by the FBR at the time of registration. It is calculated as a percentage of the car's invoice value. The percentage is determined by the engine capacity (CC).

2. **Provincial Registration Fee**
Paid to the Excise & Taxation Department of your province.
- **Punjab:** Uses the **ePay Punjab** system. Fee is approximately 2% of the value.
- **Sindh:** Uses a slab-based percentage system ranging from 1% to 5%.
- **Islamabad:** Follows federal slabs and fixed fees.

3. **Value Added Fees**
- **Automated Registration Card:** ~Rs. 1,500
- **Standard Number Plates:** ~Rs. 2,000
- **Luxury Tax:** Applicable for cars above 1500cc (imported or high-end local).

### Filer vs. Non-Filer: The Difference

| Engine CC | Filer Tax (WHT) | Non-Filer Tax (WHT) |
|-----------|-----------------|---------------------|
| Up to 1000cc | 1.0% | 3.0% |
| 1001 - 1300cc | 1.5% | 4.5% |
| 1301 - 1600cc | 2.0% | 6.0% |
| Above 2000cc | 4.0% | 12.0% |

### Steps to Register Your Vehicle

1. **Obtain the Invoice:** Get the original sales invoice and sales certificate from the manufacturer.
2. **Check your ATL Status:** Ensure you are appearing as 'Active' on the FBR website to qualify for the lower tax rate.
3. **Generate a PSID:** Use the **ePay Punjab** app (for Punjab) or the provincial Excise portal to generate a Payment Slip ID.
4. **Pay the Fee:** Pay via mobile banking or at a designated bank branch.
5. **Visit the Excise Office:** Provide your documents and get your vehicle inspected (if required).

> [!WARNING]
> **Late Registration Penalty:** Always register your car within 30 days of the invoice date. Late registration can attract heavy fines starting from Rs. 2,000 per month.
